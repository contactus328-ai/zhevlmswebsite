LMS Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about Learning Management Systems.

What is a Learning Management System (LMS)?

A Learning Management System is a digital platform used to manage, deliver, and track online courses and training programs for students and employees.

Who should use an LMS?

LMS platforms are used by coaching institutes, schools, universities, corporate training teams, and online education providers.

Is Zhev LMS white-labeled?

Yes, Zhev LMS is fully white-labeled, allowing you to use your own branding, logo, domain, and colors.

Is LMS secure?

Modern LMS platforms use cloud security, encrypted data, secure logins, and role-based access control to protect user data.

Can an LMS scale with my organization?

Yes. Cloud-based LMS platforms scale easily from small institutes to large universities and enterprises.

Does LMS work on mobile?

Yes. Zhev LMS is fully mobile-friendly and works on phones, tablets, and desktops.
